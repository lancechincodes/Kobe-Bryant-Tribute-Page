Kobe Bryant

One of the Greatest Basketball Players Ever.

Kobe Bryant is an NBA legend. He is a 5-time world champion, 18-time All-Star, 15-time member of the ALL-NBA Team, 12-time member of the All-Defensive Team, the 2008 NBA MVP, and 2-time finals MVP.

However, he is not only a celebrated winner on the the court, but he is also an icon off the court with his philantropic efforts and impact on the LA community.
